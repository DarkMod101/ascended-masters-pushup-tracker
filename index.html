Technique & Safety

Push-Up Form Tips

  • Keep your body aligned from head to heels.
  • Keep elbows controlled, not wildly flared.
  • Brace your core and glutes during every rep.
  • Control the lowering phase. Do not collapse.
  • Stop if you feel sharp wrist, shoulder, neck, or elbow pain.
